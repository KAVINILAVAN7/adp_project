Cream Biscuits


are a type of sandwich biscuit, where two biscuit cookies are filled with a sweet, creamy filling, typically made of sugar, fat (like butter or shortening), and flavorings. They are a popular snack in many parts of the world and come in a variety of flavors, such as vanilla, chocolate, strawberry, and more.

Flavors

Vanilla: The most common flavor for both the biscuit and the cream filling, offering a classic and simple taste. Chocolate: Chocolate-flavored cream or chocolate biscuits are also very popular, with a deep, rich flavor. Strawberry: Some cream biscuits are filled with a strawberry-flavored cream, adding a fruity sweetness. Other Variants: Depending on the brand and region, cream biscuits can also come in other flavors like lemon, coffee, mint, or even more exotic varieties.


Oreo: Perhaps the most well-known cream biscuit brand globally, famous for its chocolate biscuit with vanilla cream filling. Oreos are known for their distinctive flavor and are often used in various desserts. Bourbon Biscuits: A popular cream biscuit in the UK, consisting of two dark chocolate-flavored biscuits filled with a chocolate cream filling. Hide & Seek: A popular cream biscuit brand in India, often available in chocolate and vanilla flavors. Treats: A local brand offering a similar cream-filled biscuit in many regions.
